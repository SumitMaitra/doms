Workshop on Research Methodology

21th December 2025

About the Workshop:

This workshop is meant for research scholars and students who are interested in learning the commonly used tools and techniques in data science and AI for exploring patterns in the data and drawing insight from it.

Why Attend:

The workshop will enable the participants to acquire skills of formulating valid research questions and answering it using data arising out of real-life applications in social sciences and management science.
